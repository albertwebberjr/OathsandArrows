🌾 Fermentables & Raw Ingredients

Field-Cut Barley Grist

Premium malted two-row spring barley. Provides sweet, nutty notes and the vital starch foundation required for any standard tavern-style ale.

Highland Copper Malt

A drum-roasted caramel specialty grain. Imparts a brilliant, deep amber-to-copper color profile and an intensely bready aroma to the barrel.

Kiln-Baked Biscuit Grain

Heavy caramel malt derived from top-tier barley. Deepens the body and injects rich, dark biscuit profiles for hearty winter stouts.

Kiln-Charred Black Barley

Grains roasted over direct stone kilns until dark as midnight. Contributes a rich, sharp color and heavy flavor characteristic of porters.

Toasted Hearth Malt

Richly roasted grains evoking notes of dark cocoa and roasted bread. Perfect for deepening the color of brown ales and dark stouts.

🌿 Herbs, Preservatives & Botanicals

Dried Bittering Vine-Flowers

Pressed and dried bitter green hop flowers. Sharpens the tongue, balances heavy malts, and preserves the brew during long cart journeys.

Southern Sea-Merchant’s Citrus Rind

Dried, bitter citrus skins imported from foreign trading vessels. Offers a unique lemon-citrus character perfect for refreshing summer blends.

Orchard Press Apple Concentrate

Concentrate pressed from traditional orchard apples, high in tannins and tart acidity. Ideal for fermentation into sharp farmhouse ciders.

Meadow-Harvest Sweet Honey

Raw, unpasteurized honey gathered directly from sunshine orchard hives. Yields an unmistakable floral aroma perfect for your finest meads.

🏺 Fermentation Vessels & Storage

Blown-Glass Fermentation Vault

Heavy, thick-walled glass aging vessels. Allows a cellar-master to monitor the clarity and settling of sediment without opening the seal.

Ration Bottles (Thick Amber Glass)

Dark, heavy glass flasks designed to block out sunlight and prevent your stored ale from skunking. Case of 24.

Reinforced Cellar-Master’s Tub

Heavy-duty, robust fermentation and storage tank. Built with extra thick walls to preserve the deep aroma of your beverage.

Slavonian Oak Barrel (Mild Toast)

Sourced from thick-timbered old-world forests. Superior for long-term cellaring due to a tight wood grain that slowly mellows the spirits within.

🛠️ Hearth & Brew Day Gear

Blacksmith’s Heavy Boil Kettle

Thick-bottomed, heavy-gauge steel vessel built to sit directly over high heat or outdoor firepits without scorching.

The Inspector’s Gravity Float

A weighted glass tool used to measure the density and sugar content of the liquid. Necessary for calculating final proof.

Heavy Stirring Oar

Long, solid steel paddle to constantly keep thick honeys and heavy extracts moving so they do not burn on the iron bottom.

Gas-Release Trapping Valve

A simple water-trap mechanism. Allows the natural pressure of fermenting gas to safely vent while blocking sour air and flies.

Scrubbing Bristles & Wire Scourers

Long, flexible wire brushes designed to curve inside the necks of glass jugs to scrape away dried yeast cake and mold.
